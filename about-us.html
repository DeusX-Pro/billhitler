Who We Are

A Modern & Leading Industrial Company

Billhitler is a forward-thinking industrial firm headquartered in Warri, Delta State, serving clients across Nigeria’s industrial, commercial and infrastructure sectors. We specialize in the importation, exportation and supply of high-quality electrical equipment, alongside professional welding, fabrication and electrical engineering services.

Our operations are driven by a commitment to precision, safety, durability, and efficiency. From sourcing globally trusted electrical components to delivering on-site engineering and fabrication solutions, we support projects from planning to execution, ensuring reliable outcomes that meet both technical and regulatory standards.

We combine modern industrial practices, skilled manpower, and strong supplier partnerships to deliver solutions that are dependable, cost-effective, and built to last. Whether supporting large-scale industrial operations or specialized engineering needs, Billhitler stands as a trusted partner for quality-driven industrial solutions.
